Traction Application

This app aims to aid the NC Harm Reduction Coalition (NC HRC) and other groups in fighting the statewide opioid crisis. It was developed by a team of 5 junior developers in 2.5 weeks as a capstone project from Momentum's immersive coding program. It's twofold function will allow NC HRC administrators to organize community volunteers and utilize a QR code scanning technology to track the distribution of Naloxone (Narcan) kits. It vastly improves the current processess by ensuring data accuracy, efficiency, and security in data collection and analysis.
